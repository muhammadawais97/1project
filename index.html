*Quaid-e-Azam muhammad ali jinnah*

Quaid-e-Azam is a phrase in Urdu which means "the great leader"1. It is used to refer to Muhammad Ali Jinnah, who is the founder of Pakistan1. Quaid-e-Azam Day is celebrated every year on December 25 in Pakistan to celebrate Jinnah's birthday2. He is believed to be the founder of the country and the major national holiday is celebrated with a lot of events throughout the country2.
